The University of Michigan

to all who may read these letters, Greetings.

The Horace H. Rackham School of Graduate Studies

in recognition of the satisfactory fufillment of the prescribed requirements the degree of

Mechanical Engineering

With all the rights, privileges, and honors thereto pertaining here and elsewhere.

Dated at Ann Arbor, Michigan this second day of May, two thousand and thirty-two
